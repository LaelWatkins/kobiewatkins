"Journey" with The Kobie Watkins Grouptet

About this project

The "Journey" cd project is original melody and rhythm driven music, I've been writing for the past 8yrs as well as a drum exercise book.

People have asked me about my next cd project for the past 5yrs. During the years of travel and devotion to playing jazz and world music, I feel honored to share all that music and life has lead me to. My new Family wife and son (Allison & Kobie Jr). As well as some of the cool places I've lived in the United States. Several train rides through Russia, countless bus rides throughout Europe, people watching in airports, and singing inspiring original melodies into my phone for later arranging in passport check points or in the middle of the night.

This is my chance to share my Journey from the drum chair.. It's truly interesting back there.
